Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the showcase? expand_more

Early-stage startups with an MVP or live product. We welcome founders from all industries, with a focus on Tech, AI, and Social Impact.

Is there any registration fee? expand_more

No, entry is completely free for both participants and viewers as part of our mission to support the ecosystem.

How are the winners selected? expand_more

A panel of industry judges evaluates pitches based on innovation, market viability, and presentation quality.

Will the event be recorded? expand_more

Yes, the full showcase will be recorded and shared with participants and on our social channels later.
